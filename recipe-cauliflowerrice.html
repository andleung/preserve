20 min(Serves 4)


Ingredients

1 head of cauliflower
1/2 cup parsley, finely chopped
2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
Salt
Lemon juice

01.

Try cauliflower as a healthy rice alternative. When prepared this way, the cauliflower takes on a light, fluffy texture while the parsley and citrus add a bright, clean flavor.

Using a cheese grater, grate the head of cauliflower, discarding the stem. The gratings should crumble off the head and resemble rice.

02.

On a stovetop over medium heat, coat a pan with olive oil and add the grated cauliflower with a pinch of salt. Cover and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.

03.

When the cauliflower is fluffy and tender, toss with the fresh parsley and a squeeze of lemon juice, then serve.
