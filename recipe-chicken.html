1.5 hr(Serves 4)


Ingredients

1 whole chicken
1 large yellow onion, quartered
1 head of garlic
1 potato, chopped into wedges
2 large carrots, chopped into thirds
1 lemon, cut in half
1 tbsp. butter
3 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
2 sprigs of rosemary
Salt & pepper

01.

This recipe roasts a whole chicken over a bed of vegetables for an easy one pan dish.

Preheat oven to 425 degrees.

Clean chicken and remove the innards. Pat dry and rub butter into and under the skin, then season inside and out with salt and pepper. Stuff the chicken cavity with the lemon and rosemary sprigs.

02.

Place the onion, carrots, and potatoes into a large baking pan and toss with olive oil, salt and pepper. Spread the vegetables across the bottom and place the chicken directly on top.

03.

Roast in the oven until chicken temperature reaches 165 degrees, about 1 to 1.5 hours depending on the size of the bird.

Remove from oven and let rest for at least 10 minutes before serving.
