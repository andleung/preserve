30 min(Serves 2)


Ingredients

4 eggs
1 can crushed tomatoes
1/2 yellow onion, diced
1/2 bell pepper, diced
3 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
2 tbsp. feta cheese
1 tsp. smoked paprika

Salt, pepper, and cilantro to garnish.

01.

These eggs can be eaten right out of the pan with a side of fresh, rustic bread to soak up the sauce. Itâ€™s the perfect breakfast comfort food.

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. On the stovetop, place a cast iron skillet over medium heat and add olive oil, onions, and bell peppers. Cook until tender, about 5-10 minutes.

Add the canned tomatoes and bring to a simmer for an additional 5 minutes or until sauce has thickened.

02.

Make an indentation in the sauce with a spoon and gently crack an egg into it. Repeat with the remaining eggs. Dust with smoked paprika and crumble the feta over the top.

Place the skillet into the oven and bake until eggs are firm but still runny inside, about 15 minutes.

03.

Remove skillet from the oven and season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh chopped cilantro.

Eggs and sauce can be shared directly out of the pan (taking care to avoid hot surfaces), or scooped into individual bowls.
