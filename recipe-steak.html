10 min(Serves 2)


Ingredients

2 steaks of your choice
2 sprigs rosemary
4 sprigs thyme
1/4 stick butter
Salt & pepper

01.

Prior to cooking, remove steak from the fridge, covered, and bring to room temperature.

Preheat the oven to 425 degrees. On the stovetop place a cast iron skillet over medium to high heat.

02.

Once the pan is very hot, add butter, herbs, and steak. Sear for about 2 minutes on each side, basting with the butter and herbs.

Transfer the entire pan to the oven. Be careful as the pan will be very hot.

03.

Bake about 5 minutes for a rare steak, or a bit longer depending on desired level of doneness.

Remove steak from the oven and transfer to a cutting board. Season all over with salt and pepper. Let sit at least 5-10 minutes before serving to let the juices settle.
